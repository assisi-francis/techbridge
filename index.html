The Process

How TechBridge Works

Getting started is simple. From your first request to ongoing support, we make the journey seamless.

01
Step 1

Make Your Request

Fill out our simple request form online or give us a call. Tell us your institution's name, size, and technology needs. No commitment required at this stage — just a conversation.

📝
02
Step 2

Needs Assessment & Proposal

Our team conducts a thorough needs assessment for your institution. We then prepare a detailed proposal — including recommended devices, payment plan options, and total cost breakdown.

📊
03
Step 3

Sign Agreement & First Payment

Once you're satisfied with the proposal, we sign a formal agreement and you make your first instalment payment. This kicks off your TechBridge journey.

✍️
04
Step 4

Delivery & Training Begins

Your pre-configured laptops are delivered to your location. Our team conducts hands-on training for teachers, staff, or beneficiaries to ensure everyone hits the ground running.

🚚
05
Step 5

Monthly Payments & Ongoing Support

Enjoy predictable monthly payments while our support team stays available for technical assistance. We remain your partner from day one through the completion of your plan.
